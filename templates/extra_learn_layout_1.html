What is a spectrogram?

A spectrogram visualizes audio by recording the pitch (y-axis) and intensity (darkness) of a sound over time (x-axis).
When is the pitch increasing (rising) or decreasing (falling)?
Does the song have one part that might be repeated...
or multiple distinct parts?
What is the quality of the sound? Are the notes clear (like a flute or whistle)...
or harsh/scratchy?
